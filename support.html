Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a team?

Sign in, tap the + button on the Teams screen, choose your sport, and give your team a name. Share the Team ID with your players so they can request to join.

How do players join a team?

Players tap "Join a Team" on the Teams screen, enter the Team ID provided by their coach, and submit a join request. The coach approves or denies from the team detail screen.

Can players see their rankings?

No. Rankings are private and visible to coaches only. Players cannot see their own score or anyone else's.

How do I remove ads?

Tap the banner ad or go to your profile and select "Remove Ads" to make a one-time in-app purchase. This is tied to your account and survives reinstalls.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data including availability records, lineup assignments, and drop-in history. Email us if you need assistance.

What sports are supported?

Sport Rosters supports 21 sports including Ice Hockey, Football/Soccer, Basketball, Dragon Boating, Volleyball, Baseball, Softball, and more.
