Sport Rosters

All-in-one team management for coaches and players across 21 sports.

Features

📅

Scheduling & Availability

Schedule games and practices. Players RSVP with a tap — know who's showing up before game day.

📋

Lineup Builder

Build lineups manually or auto-generate balanced rosters based on rankings and position preferences.

🚣

Dragon Boat Seating

Automatic weight-balanced boat seating chart with left/right and front/back balance indicators.

🏒

Drop-In Sessions

Open a session, players sign up, and the app auto-generates balanced teams on the spot.

🏆

Sub-Teams

Split your roster into balanced sub-teams using smart snake-draft with a single tap.

🔒

Private Rankings

Player rankings are coach-only and never visible to players.
